About

This is a silly web interface to assign random roles for the videogame Secret World Legends (SWL), a MMORPG by Funcom.

Updated for SWL based on the TSW Roll for Role tool by AliceOrwell.

Instructions

  1. Populate the Roles section by selecting a preset from the Presets dropdown.
  2. Copy & paste your rolls from your SWL chat window into the lefthand Rolls box.
  3. Verify rolls were processed correctly by examining the sorted roll results in the righthand Rolls window.
  4. Your roll assignments will now have appeared in the Output box.

Notes

If none of the presets are suitable for your needs you choose to edit an existing one or start from scratch using the Custom preset. When you have decided on what roles you want you will need to manually trigger your roll assignments by clicking the Process button.
